Patricia Manasan

Data Scientist | Leader | Speaker

Patricia is a pioneering graduate of the Master of Science in Data Science program of the Asian Institute of Management, graduating with distinction. She took her Bachelor of Science in Applied Physics at the University of the Philippines, garnering cum laude and best graduate. She has over 12 years of leadership experience, most of which was spent heading operations in an online sportsbetting company where she managed risk, maximized profit through trading, and built the foundations of data science in her teams.
She is currently Senior Assistant Vice President and Data Scientist at Security Bank, a top universal bank in the Philippines. She is passionate about artificial intelligence, machine learning, and neural networks. She is also into reading, playing games, drinking coffee like there's no tomorrow, and conversing with cats.
